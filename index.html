About Me

I'm a backend engineer. I've spent the last seven years at a healthcare-data company doing client-facing implementation work. Partner integrations, multi-stage pipelines, HIPAA-regulated data in Java and SQL.

What I enjoy most is the client side of the work. Sitting with a client, understanding what they actually need, and building toward the real solution rather than the literal ask. The work I'm proudest of is making a complicated system understandable to the people who had to use it.

Throughout my career I've worked in production systems under HIPAA compliance and HITRUST audit pressure. That shaped how I think about backend engineering. I deliver carefully, I'm explicit about how failures get handled, and I focus on building systems the next engineer can actually read months later.

Currently looking for a backend role doing integration-heavy work with regulated or high-volume data.
