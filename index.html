What I Do

I spent seven years at Smart Data Solutions working on client-facing implementations, partner integrations, and multi-stage pipelines with HIPAA-regulated data in Java and SQL.

Throughout my career I've worked in production systems under HIPAA compliance and HITRUST audit pressure. That shaped how I think about backend engineering.
