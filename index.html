What I Do

I've spent the last seven years at Smart Data Solutions working on client-facing implementations, partner integrations, and multi-stage pipelines with HIPAA-regulated data in Java and SQL.

What I enjoy most is the client side of the work. Sitting with a client, understanding what they actually need, and building toward real solutions. The work I'm proudest of is making a complicated system understandable for the people who use it.

Throughout my career I've worked in production systems under HIPAA compliance and HITRUST audit pressure. That shaped how I think about backend engineering.
