About Me

Outside of work I'm usually with my wife Rebecca, our two cats Eva and Zed, and our 1-year-old golden retriever Jax.

You might find me hiking around the Twin Cities metro parks most weeks. Usually Jax leads the way, dragging Rebecca and me along. It's the easiest way to step away from a screen for a few hours.

Beyond that I unwind with video games. My main rotation includes WoW, LoL, Tarkov, PoE, Dark Souls, Zelda, Metroid, and plenty more. A good mix of competitive and casual games to get lost in.
